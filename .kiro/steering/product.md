# TripSlip Platform

TripSlip is a digital field trip management platform that connects venues, schools, teachers, and parents through a unified ecosystem. The platform streamlines the entire field trip experience from planning to execution.

## Core Applications

- **Landing App** (`tripslip.com`) - Public marketing website
- **Venue App** (`venue.tripslip.com`) - Venue management and experience creation
- **School App** (`school.tripslip.com`) - School/district administration
- **Teacher App** (`teacher.tripslip.com`) - Trip planning and roster management  
- **Parent App** (`parent.tripslip.com`) - Permission slips and payments

## Key Features

- Multi-application architecture with shared backend
- Flexible authentication (required for venues, optional for teachers/parents)
- Payment processing with Stripe integration
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, Arabic with RTL)
- Document management with PDF generation and secure storage
- FERPA compliance with audit trails and data privacy controls
- Comprehensive notification system (email, SMS, in-app)

## Target Users

- **Venues**: Museums, zoos, educational centers offering field trip experiences
- **Schools**: Districts and administrators managing field trip policies
- **Teachers**: Planning trips, managing rosters, tracking permissions
- **Parents**: Providing consent, making payments, staying informed
