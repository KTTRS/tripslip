## Summary: Key Insights Across Personas

### Venue Managers
- Range from tech-resistant to power users
- All want to reduce administrative burden
- Need to prove ROI and track revenue
- Want to fill capacity and reduce no-shows

### School Administrators
- Focused on budget, safety, and educational value
- Need visibility and control
- Want data for decision-making
- Range from single-school to district-level

### Teachers
- Passionate about student experiences
- Overwhelmed by logistics
- Need simple, time-saving tools
- Serve diverse student populations

### Parents
- Extremely diverse in tech literacy and language
- All want what's best for their kids
- Need mobile-friendly, accessible tools
- Range from hands-off to highly involved

These personas should guide every design and development decision to ensure TripSlip serves all users effectively.
