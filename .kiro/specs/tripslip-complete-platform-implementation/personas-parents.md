## Parent Personas

### Persona 11: Maria Gonzalez - Working Single Mom
**Demographics:**
- Age: 34
- Location: Miami, FL
- Role: Retail Manager
- Children: 2 (ages 7 and 10)
- Tech Savviness: Medium
- Primary Language: Spanish

**Background:**
Maria works full-time and juggles two kids' schedules. She wants her children to have enriching experiences but struggles with time and money. She primarily uses her phone for everything.

**Goals:**
- Quickly review and sign permission slips
- Understand trip costs and payment options
- Get reminders so she doesn't miss deadlines
- Communicate with teachers easily
- Access everything on her phone

**Pain Points:**
- Doesn't have time to check email regularly
- Prefers Spanish language communications
- Worried about affording trip costs
- Forgets to return paper permission slips
- Needs flexible payment options

**Tech Habits:**
- Mobile-first (rarely uses computer)
- Prefers SMS over email
- Uses WhatsApp for communication
- Needs simple, fast interfaces

**Quote:** "I want my kids to go on every trip, but I need it to be quick and easy. I don't have time to deal with complicated forms or chase down paperwork."

**Demographics:**
- Age: 40 and 38
- Location: San Francisco, CA
- Roles: Software Engineer and Marketing Director
- Children: 3 (ages 6, 9, 12)
- Tech Savviness: Very High

- Manage all three kids' trips in one place
- Pay quickly and securely online
- Get real-time updates and notifications

- Track spending across all children

**Pain Points:**

- Different teachers use different processes
- Want consolidated view of all trips
- Frustrated by paper forms and checks
- Need reminders for multiple deadlines

**Tech Habits:**
- Expect app-like experiences
- Use Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Want push notifications
- Value clean, modern interfaces

**Quote:** "We're happy to support our kids' education, but we need a system that respects our time. One account, all kids, all trips, done."

### Persona 15: Karen and Steve Mitchell - Helicopter Parents
**Demographics:**
- Age: 45 and 47
- Location: Westchester, NY
- Roles: Attorney and Financial Advisor
- Children: 1 (age 10)
- Tech Savviness: High

**Background:**
The Mitchells are highly involved in their son's education and have high expectations. They research everything thoroughly and ask lots of questions. They're willing to pay premium prices for quality.

**Goals:**
- Thoroughly vet every trip for safety and value
- Communicate directly with teachers and venues
- Understand exactly what their son will experience
- Ensure educational standards are met
- Get detailed information about supervision

**Pain Points:**
- Not enough information provided upfront
- Want to research venues before approving
- Concerned about safety and supervision ratios
- Frustrated by lack of transparency
- Want ability to ask questions easily

**Tech Habits:**
- Research everything online
- Read reviews extensively
- Expect detailed information
- Value transparency and communication

**Quote:** "We need to know exactly what our son will be doing, who's supervising, and what educational value he'll get. Don't just tell us to trust you—show us."
