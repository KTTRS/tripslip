## Financial Projections

### Revenue Model

TripSlip uses a **hybrid transaction + freemium subscription model** designed to maximize adoption while capturing value from successful bookings.

#### Primary Revenue: Transaction Fees

**Booking Commission:**
- Platform fee: 3-5% commission on all venue bookings processed through TripSlip
- Applied to total booking value before Stripe fees
- Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe Connect pass-through)
- **Total venue cost: 5.9-7.9% + $0.30 per transaction**

**Example Transaction:**
- Booking: 30 students × $12 = $360
- TripSlip commission (4% average): $14.40
- Stripe fee (2.9% + $0.30): $10.74
- Venue receives: $334.86 (93%)
- TripSlip revenue: $14.40

**Transaction Volume Projections:**
- Medium venue: 1,000 bookings/year × $360 avg = $360K volume
- TripSlip revenue (4% avg): $14,400/venue/year from transactions
- High-volume venue: 3,000 bookings/year × $400 avg = $1.2M volume
- TripSlip revenue: $48,000/venue/year from transactions

**Why this works:**
- Venues only pay when they get bookings (no upfront risk)
- Aligns TripSlip's success with venue success
- Lower barrier to entry than pure subscription
- Scales naturally with venue growth
- Competitive with other booking platforms (Eventbrite: 3.5%, Bookeo: similar)

## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**Risk 1: Chicken-and-Egg Problem**
- **Description:** Need venues to attract schools, need schools to attract venues
- **Probability:** High (70%)
- **Impact:** Critical
- **Mitigation:**
  - Launch in dense urban markets (SF, NYC, Chicago)
  - Sign anchor venues first (top 10 in each city)
  - Offer free trials to first 100 schools
  - Provide value even with partial adoption
- **Residual risk:** Medium

**Risk 2: Slow School Adoption**
- **Description:** Schools resistant to change, long sales cycles
- **Probability:** Medium (50%)
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Teacher-first strategy (free, viral)
  - Show clear ROI (budget compliance)
  - Partner with school associations
  - Provide free training and support
- **Residual risk:** Low

**Risk 3: Competitive Response**
- **Description:** Incumbents add field trip features
- **Probability:** Medium (40%)
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Move fast, build network effects
  - Focus on UX and parent experience
  - Dual B2B + B2C model (hard to replicate)
  - Build data moat
- **Residual risk:** Medium

**Risk 4: Regulatory Changes**
- **Description:** New education data privacy laws
- **Probability:** Low (20%)
- **Impact:** Medium
- **Mitigation:**
  - Build compliance from day one
  - Monitor regulatory landscape
  - Maintain legal counsel
  - Over-comply with current standards
- **Residual risk:** Low

**Risk 5: Economic Downturn**
- **Description:** Schools cut field trip budgets
- **Probability:** Medium (30%)
- **Impact:** High
- **Mitigation:**
  - Show cost savings (not just convenience)
  - Emphasize budget compliance features
  - Expand B2C revenue (less cyclical)
  - Offer flexible pricing
- **Residual risk:** Medium
