# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 4, 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 4, 2026

## Introduction

TripSlip ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, teachers, schools, and venues using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Student Information (FERPA Protected)

We collect and process student information only as necessary to provide our services:

- Student name
- Grade level
- Parent/guardian contact information
- Emergency contact information
- Medical information (if provided)
- Permission slip signatures
- Trip participation records

**FERPA Compliance:** All student information is protected under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and is treated as education records.

### Parent/Guardian Information

- Name and contact information
- Email address and phone number
- Payment information (processed by Stripe)
- Digital signatures
- Communication preferences

### Teacher Information

- Name and email address
- School affiliation
- Trip planning information
- Communication with parents and venues

### School/District Information

- School name and address
- Administrator contact information
- District policies and settings
- Budget and financial information

### Venue Information

- Venue name and description
- Contact information
- Banking information (processed by Stripe)
- Experience details and pricing
- Booking information

### Automatically Collected Information

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage data and analytics
- Cookies and similar technologies

## How We Use Your Information

### To Provide Services

- Process permission slips and payments
- Facilitate communication between parties
- Manage trip bookings and logistics
- Generate reports and analytics
- Provide customer support

### To Improve Services

- Analyze usage patterns
- Identify and fix technical issues
- Develop new features
- Enhance user experience

### To Communicate

- Send transactional emails and SMS
- Provide trip updates and reminders
- Respond to inquiries
- Send important notices

### Legal Compliance

- Comply with FERPA requirements
- Respond to legal requests
- Enforce our terms of service
- Protect rights and safety

## Information Sharing

### We Do Not Sell Your Information

We never sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Service Providers

We share information with trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform:

- **Supabase:** Database and authentication
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **SendGrid/Resend:** Email delivery
- **Twilio:** SMS delivery
- **Sentry:** Error monitoring

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for specified purposes.

### School Officials

Student information may be shared with authorized school officials who have legitimate educational interests.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

### With Consent

We may share information with your explicit consent or at your direction.

## Data Security

### Security Measures

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication
- Intrusion detection and prevention
- Regular backups
- Incident response procedures

### Payment Security

All payment information is processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We never store credit card information on our servers.

## Data Retention

### Student Records

Student education records are retained for 7 years after the student's last activity, in compliance with FERPA requirements.

### Other Information

- Payment records: 7 years (tax requirements)
- Communication logs: 3 years
- Analytics data: 2 years
- Account information: Until account deletion

### Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their data at any time. We will delete or anonymize data within 30 days of request, except where retention is required by law.

## Your Rights

### Access and Correction

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request data export
- Request data deletion

### FERPA Rights

Parents and eligible students have the right to:
- Inspect and review education records
- Request amendment of records
- Consent to disclosure of records
- File complaints with the Department of Education

### Communication Preferences

You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time through account settings or unsubscribe links.

### Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track signals and do not track users across third-party websites.

## Children's Privacy

### COPPA Compliance

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

### Parental Consent

Schools act as agents for parents in providing consent for the collection of student information for educational purposes.

### Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:
- Review their child's information
- Request correction or deletion
- Refuse further collection or use
- Receive notification of data breaches

## Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies

We use essential cookies necessary for the platform to function:
- Authentication and session management
- Security and fraud prevention
- Load balancing

### Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with our platform. You may opt out through browser settings.

### Third-Party Cookies

Some third-party services (Stripe, analytics) may set their own cookies. Please review their privacy policies.

## International Data Transfers

Our services are hosted in the United States. If you access our services from outside the U.S., your information will be transferred to and processed in the U.S.

## California Privacy Rights

### CCPA Compliance

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what information is collected
- Right to know if information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt out of sale (we do not sell information)
- Right to deletion
- Right to non-discrimination

### Shine the Light

California residents may request information about disclosure of personal information to third parties for direct marketing purposes.
