# Privacy Policy Summary

## Data Collection
We collect:
- Student names and grades (with parental consent)
- Parent contact information
- Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)
- Trip and permission slip data

## Data Usage
Your data is used to:
- Facilitate field trip planning and management
- Process payments securely
- Send notifications about trips
- Comply with FERPA requirements

## Data Protection
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Secure transmission (TLS 1.3)
- Row Level Security (RLS) enforced
- Regular security audits
- FERPA-compliant audit logging

## Data Retention
- Active trip data: Retained while trip is active
- Historical data: 7 years for audit compliance
- Audit logs: 90 days (critical errors: 1 year)

## Your Rights
- Access your data
- Export your data
- Request data deletion
- Opt out of SMS notifications
