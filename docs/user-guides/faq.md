# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is TripSlip?

TripSlip is a digital platform that streamlines field trip management for venues, schools, teachers, and parents. It handles everything from trip planning to permission slips to payments.

### Who can use TripSlip?

- **Venues:** Museums, zoos, and educational centers offering field trips
- **Schools:** Districts and administrators managing field trip policies
- **Teachers:** Educators planning and managing field trips
- **Parents:** Parents providing permission and making payments

### Is TripSlip free?

- Teachers and parents can use TripSlip for free
- Venues pay a small platform fee on bookings
- Schools can use basic features for free, with premium features available

### What languages does TripSlip support?

TripSlip supports English, Spanish (Español), and Arabic (العربية) with full right-to-left (RTL) support.

## For Teachers

### Do I need an account to use TripSlip?

No, you can plan trips and manage permission slips without creating an account. However, an account lets you save trips and access them later.

### How do I create a field trip?

1. Visit teacher.tripslip.com
2. Browse experiences or search by location
3. Select an experience and date
4. Add your student roster
5. Generate permission slips

### How do I add students to my roster?

You can either:
- Enter student information manually
- Upload a CSV file with student data
- Import from your school's system (if integrated)

### What if parents don't receive permission slips?

- Verify email addresses are correct
- Ask parents to check spam/junk folders
- Resend the permission slip
- Provide the direct magic link

### Can I track who has signed permission slips?

Yes, your dashboard shows real-time status of all permission slips including signed, pending, and paid status.

### What if I need to cancel a trip?

You can cancel trips from your dashboard. All parents will be automatically notified, and refunds will be processed according to your school's policy.

## For Parents

### Do I need to create an account?

No, you access permission slips through a secure magic link sent to your email. No account or password needed.

### How do I sign a permission slip?

1. Click the magic link in your email
2. Review trip details
3. Provide required information
4. Sign digitally
5. Submit

### Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. TripSlip never stores your payment card information.

### Can multiple people pay for one trip?

Yes, TripSlip supports split payments. Multiple parents or guardians can contribute to the trip cost.

### What if I can't afford the trip?

Contact your child's teacher or school about financial assistance programs that may be available.

### Can I decline permission for my child?

Yes, you can decline permission through the permission slip. Your child's teacher will be notified.

### What if the magic link doesn't work?

- The link may have expired - request a new one from the teacher
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser cache
- Contact the teacher for assistance

## For Venues

### How do I get started as a venue?

1. Visit venue.tripslip.com
2. Create an account
3. Complete your venue profile
4. Create your first experience
5. Set up Stripe Connect for payments

### How do I receive payments?

Payments are automatically transferred to your bank account via Stripe Connect within 2-7 business days after the trip occurs.

### What fees does TripSlip charge?

TripSlip charges a small platform fee on each booking. Stripe also charges payment processing fees. All fees are clearly disclosed before you confirm pricing.

### Can I offer discounts?

Yes, you can create promotional codes, group discounts, and special pricing for specific schools or districts.

### How do I manage my calendar?

Use the calendar feature to:
- Set available dates and times
- Block out unavailable dates
- Set booking lead times
- Manage capacity

### What if I need to cancel a booking?

Contact the teacher as soon as possible. Refunds are processed according to your cancellation policy. Frequent cancellations may affect your venue rating.

## For Schools

### How does TripSlip help school administrators?

TripSlip provides:
- Oversight of all school trips
- Budget tracking and management
- Trip approval workflows
- Compliance and reporting tools
- Teacher management

### Can we set approval requirements?

Yes, you can configure:
- Which trips require approval
- Budget limits
- Lead time requirements
- Chaperone ratios
- Other policies

### How do we track our field trip budget?

The school dashboard shows:
- Total budget vs. spending
- Spending by teacher/department
- Upcoming trip costs
- Payment collection status

### Can we integrate with our student information system?

Yes, TripSlip offers integration options with major student information systems. Contact us for details.

### How is student data protected?

TripSlip is FERPA compliant with:
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Role-based access controls
- Audit logging
- Regular security audits
- Data retention policies

## Security and Privacy

### Is TripSlip FERPA compliant?

Yes, TripSlip fully complies with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Student data is protected as education records.

### How is my data protected?

- All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
- Role-based access controls
- Continuous monitoring

### Who can see student information?

Only authorized parties:
- The student's teacher
- School administrators with proper permissions
- Parents/guardians of the student
- TripSlip staff (limited, for support only)

### Can I delete my data?

Yes, you can request deletion of your data at any time. We will delete or anonymize data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

### Do you sell user data?

No, we never sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Payments

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### Are payments secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through Stripe, which is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified - the highest level of payment security.

### When are payments processed?

Payments are processed immediately when submitted. Funds are transferred to venues after the trip occurs.

### What is your refund policy?

Refund policies are set by individual schools and venues. Contact the organizing teacher for refund requests.

### Can I get a receipt?

Yes, you receive an email receipt immediately after payment. You can also download receipts from your payment confirmation page.
