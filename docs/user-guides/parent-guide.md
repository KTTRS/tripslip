# Parent App User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to TripSlip! This guide will help you navigate the parent portal to view, sign, and pay for your child's field trip permission slips.

## Viewing Permission Slips

1. Log in to the Parent App at `parent.tripslip.com`
2. You'll see a list of all permission slips for your children
3. Click on any slip to view details including:
   - Trip name and destination
   - Date and time
   - Cost breakdown
   - Required forms

## Signing Permission Slips

1. Open the permission slip you want to sign
2. Review all trip details carefully
3. Click "Sign Permission Slip"
4. Draw your signature using your mouse or touchscreen
5. Click "Submit Signature"

## Making Payments

### Full Payment
1. After signing, click "Pay Now"
2. Enter your payment information
3. Review the total amount
4. Click "Complete Payment"

### Split Payment
1. Click "Split Payment" instead of "Pay Now"
2. Enter the amount you want to pay
3. Complete your portion of the payment
4. Other parents can pay the remaining balance

### Add-Ons
- Select optional add-ons (lunch, souvenirs, etc.)
- The total will update automatically
- Add-ons can be selected before payment

## Payment History

View all your past payments:
1. Click "Payment History" in the menu
2. See all transactions with dates and amounts
3. Download receipts for your records

## Notifications

You'll receive notifications via:
- Email for new permission slips
- SMS reminders (if opted in)
- In-app notifications for payment confirmations

## FAQ

**Q: Can I sign a slip without paying immediately?**
A: Yes! You can sign first and pay later before the deadline.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through Stripe.

**Q: Can I get a refund if the trip is cancelled?**
A: Yes, refunds are automatically processed if a trip is cancelled.
