# School App User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to TripSlip! This guide will help school administrators manage teachers, approve trips, and track budgets.

## Inviting Teachers

1. Log in to the School App at `school.tripslip.com`
2. Click "Invite Teacher"
3. Enter teacher's email address
4. Click "Send Invitation"
5. Teacher receives email with invitation link

### Managing Invitations
- View all sent invitations
- Resend invitations if needed
- Revoke pending invitations

## Viewing School Trips

See all trips from your school's teachers:
- Filter by status (draft, pending, approved, active, completed)
- Search by trip name, teacher, or venue
- View trip details including costs and student counts

## Approving Trips

1. Click on a trip with "Pending Approval" status
2. Review trip details:
   - Destination and date
   - Number of students
   - Total cost
   - Teacher information
3. Click "Approve" or "Request Revision"
4. Teacher receives notification of decision

## Budget Tracking

Monitor school-wide field trip spending:
- **Total Budget**: All planned trips
- **Spent Budget**: Approved and active trips
- **Remaining Budget**: Available funds

### Budget by Teacher
- See spending breakdown by teacher
- View number of trips per teacher
- Track individual teacher budgets

### Budget by Status
- See costs by trip status
- Monitor pending vs. approved spending
- Plan future budget allocations

## Reports

Generate reports for:
- Monthly trip activity
- Budget utilization
- Teacher participation
- Student participation rates

## Settings

Configure school-wide settings:
- Budget limits
- Approval requirements
- Notification preferences
- School information
