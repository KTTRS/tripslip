# Venue App User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to TripSlip! This guide will help you create experiences, manage bookings, and track revenue.

## Creating an Experience

1. Log in to the Venue App at `venue.tripslip.com`
2. Click "Create Experience"
3. Fill in experience details:
   - Experience name
   - Description
   - Duration
   - Age range
   - Pricing tiers
   - Maximum capacity
4. Upload photos
5. Click "Publish Experience"

## Managing Availability

### Setting Available Dates
1. Open your experience
2. Click "Manage Availability"
3. Select available dates on the calendar
4. Set capacity for each date
5. Click "Save"

### Blocking Dates
1. Click "Block Dates"
2. Select dates to block
3. Add reason (optional)
4. Click "Confirm"

## Managing Bookings

View all bookings in one place:
- **Pending**: Awaiting your confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Approved and scheduled
- **Cancelled**: Cancelled bookings

### Confirming Bookings
1. Click on a pending booking
2. Review trip details
3. Click "Confirm Booking"
4. Teacher receives confirmation notification

### Cancelling Bookings
1. Click on a booking
2. Click "Cancel Booking"
3. Provide reason
4. Click "Confirm Cancellation"

## Financial Dashboard

Track your revenue:
- Total revenue
- Confirmed revenue
- Pending revenue
- Upcoming payouts

### Payout Schedule
- Payouts are processed weekly
- View upcoming payout dates and amounts
- Download payout reports

## Capacity Management

The system automatically:
- Tracks available slots
- Prevents overbooking
- Shows capacity warnings
- Updates availability in real-time
