# Parent User Guide

**TripSlip Parent App** - Complete guide for parents managing permission slips and payments

## Getting Started

### Accessing Permission Slips

1. Check your email for message from your child's teacher
2. Click the secure magic link
3. No account or password needed
4. Access permission slip instantly

### Email Not Received?

- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address with teacher
- Request link to be resent
- Contact teacher for direct link

## Reviewing Trip Details

### Trip Information

Permission slip shows:
- Trip destination and venue
- Date and time
- Educational purpose
- Cost breakdown
- What to bring
- Emergency contact requirements

### Understanding Costs

- Base trip cost per student
- Optional add-ons (lunch, materials, etc.)
- Total amount due
- Payment deadline
- Payment methods accepted

## Signing Permission Slips

### Step 1: Review Information

- Read all trip details carefully
- Review emergency contact information
- Note any special instructions
- Check cost and payment requirements

### Step 2: Provide Information

- Confirm student information
- Update emergency contacts if needed
- Add any special needs or accommodations
- Provide medical information if required

### Step 3: Sign

**Digital Signature Options:**
- Draw signature with mouse/finger
- Type your name
- Upload signature image

**Requirements:**
- Full legal name
- Date automatically recorded
- Cannot be changed after submission

### Step 4: Submit

- Review all information
- Click "Submit Permission Slip"
- Receive confirmation email
- Proceed to payment if required

## Making Payments

### Payment Process

1. After signing permission slip, click "Proceed to Payment"
2. Enter payment information securely
3. Review payment amount
4. Submit payment
5. Receive payment confirmation

### Payment Methods

- Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit card
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### Payment Security

- All payments processed by Stripe
- PCI-DSS compliant
- No payment information stored by TripSlip
- Secure encryption for all transactions

### Payment Confirmation

- Immediate email confirmation
- Receipt available for download
- Payment status visible on permission slip
- Teacher notified of payment

## Split Payments

### How It Works

Multiple parents/guardians can contribute to trip cost:

1. First parent signs permission slip
2. Selects "Split Payment"
3. Enters amount to pay
4. Shares link with other contributors
5. Each person pays their portion
6. Trip approved when fully paid

### Managing Split Payments

- View total amount paid
- See remaining balance
- Track who has contributed
- Receive notification when fully paid

## FAQ

**Do I need to create an account?**
No, you access permission slips via secure magic links. No account or password needed.

**How long is the magic link valid?**
Magic links remain valid until the trip date passes or the permission slip is submitted.

**Can I sign permission slips on my phone?**
Yes, TripSlip works on all devices including smartphones and tablets.

**What if I don't have a credit card?**
Contact your child's teacher about alternative payment arrangements.

**Can I get a refund?**
Refund policies are set by the school/teacher. Contact them directly for refund requests.

**Is my payment information secure?**
Yes, all payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. TripSlip never stores your payment information.

**What if I have multiple children on the same trip?**
You'll receive separate permission slips for each child. Each must be signed individually.

**Can I print the permission slip?**
Yes, you can download and print a PDF copy for your records after submission.
