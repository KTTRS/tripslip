# Teacher App User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to TripSlip! This guide will help you plan field trips, manage student rosters, and track permission slips.

## Creating a Trip

1. Log in to the Teacher App at `teacher.tripslip.com`
2. Click "Create New Trip"
3. Fill in trip details:
   - Trip name
   - Destination venue
   - Date (must be at least 2 weeks in advance)
   - Number of students
   - Cost per student
4. Click "Create Trip"

## Managing Student Rosters

### Adding Students Manually
1. Open your trip
2. Click "Manage Roster"
3. Click "Add Student"
4. Enter student name, grade, and parent contact info
5. Click "Save"

### Importing from CSV
1. Click "Import CSV"
2. Download the template if needed
3. Upload your completed CSV file
4. Review and confirm the import

### Exporting Roster
1. Click "Export Roster"
2. Choose CSV format
3. Save the file to your computer

## Generating Permission Slips

1. Once your roster is complete, click "Generate Permission Slips"
2. Slips are automatically created for all students
3. Parents receive email notifications with links to sign

## Tracking Permission Slip Status

View real-time status for each student:
- **Pending**: Not yet signed
- **Signed**: Signed but not paid
- **Paid**: Fully complete

The dashboard shows:
- Total students
- Number of signed slips
- Number of paid slips
- Outstanding payments

## Trip Statistics

View comprehensive trip statistics:
- Total cost
- Amount collected
- Outstanding balance
- Completion percentage

## Cancelling a Trip

1. Open the trip
2. Click "Cancel Trip"
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Refunds are automatically initiated for all paid slips
5. Parents receive cancellation notifications
